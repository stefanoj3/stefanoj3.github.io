Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How much does a photoshoot cost?

Photoshoot pricing is customized based on your business's specific requirements, the type of shots needed, and the duration of service. To provide you with an accurate quote, I invite you to contact me for a complimentary, no-obligation consultation where we can work together to determine the ideal solution for your needs.

Do you only do photoshoots or also videos?

In addition to professional photography, I also create compelling video content designed to capture the atmosphere and identity of your business. Many clients choose to combine both services to achieve a comprehensive and cohesive visual narrative across all their digital channels.

Are the photos you take suitable for social media and websites?

Absolutely. Every image is delivered in formats and resolutions optimized for both your website—ensuring maximum quality and fast loading speeds—and for social media platforms, providing immediate visual impact on Instagram, Facebook, and other channels.

Can a photoshoot really help me attract more customers?

Professional photography is one of the most effective investments for business growth. High-quality, professional images project trust and value, enhancing your brand's perception. This translates to improved online reputation and, consequently, increased direct bookings and revenue.

Do your marketing services include social media management?

Yes, I offer comprehensive services that include developing editorial strategies and producing custom visual content. The objective is to keep your social channels consistently updated, engaging, and professional to attract new customers while building loyalty with existing ones.

What kind of equipment do you use?

I use professional Sony cameras paired with a comprehensive selection of high-quality lenses from Sony, Sigma, and Tamron to ensure maximum sharpness and detail in every shot. For videography, I utilize DJI drones and Hohem gimbals—tools that enable me to capture smooth, dynamic footage from unique perspectives. Additionally, for projects requiring a vintage aesthetic, I employ Canon analog film cameras to achieve a distinctive and timeless effect.

Do you also help me define my brand's visual style?

Absolutely. Establishing a strong visual identity is the foundation of all my projects. Before every photoshoot or video production, I dedicate time to strategic consultation to deeply understand your story, values, and objectives. The goal extends beyond capturing beautiful images—it's about creating visuals that authentically represent you and differentiate you within your industry.
